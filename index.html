Common questions.

What is the GetNetworked public marketplace? +
Every event you publish on GetNetworked is automatically listed in our public marketplace — a searchable, location-based directory of professional events open to the GetNetworked community. Attendees browsing the marketplace can discover your event, RSVP, and purchase tickets directly. This gives your events organic reach beyond your own network, on every plan including Community — no extra cost, no boosting required.
Is hosting really free? +
Yes. Creating an account and hosting free events costs nothing on the Community plan. You only need a paid plan (Growth or Enterprise) to unlock paid ticketing, custom branding, analytics, and advanced features.
Who pays the ticket fee — me or my attendees? +
By default, attendees pay the platform fee on top of your ticket price — you always receive 100% of your face value. A $10 ticket costs the attendee $12.00. You can choose to absorb the fee in your event settings.
What is the Stripe processing fee? +
Stripe charges 2.9% + $0.30 per paid transaction for credit card processing. This is a standard payment processing fee — GetNetworked does not take any cut from the host. You always receive the full face value of your ticket price.
Is there a fee on free events? +
No. Free events cost nothing to host or attend. GetNetworked only earns when a paid ticket is sold.
What's the difference between transactional and campaign messages? +
Transactional messages are sent automatically by the platform — RSVP confirmations, event reminders (24hr and 1hr before), ticket receipts, and waitlist notifications. These are free and unlimited on every plan, including Community. Campaign messages are host-initiated — event announcements, marketing blasts, post-event follow-ups, and newsletters. These count against your monthly credit allocation. Host controls to customize or disable transactional messages are coming soon.
What happens when I exceed my campaign SMS or email limit? +
Overage rates apply automatically to campaign messages. SMS: Growth $0.018/SMS · Enterprise $0.012/SMS. Email: Growth $0.0008/email · Enterprise $0.0006/email. Alternatively, pre-purchase bulk add-ons at discounted rates — SMS from $19 (1,000) to $549 (50,000), email from $9 (10,000) to $299 (500,000). All credits roll over 90 days and never expire. Transactional messages are never affected by credit limits.
Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime? +
Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades apply at end of billing period. Annual plans lock in the discounted rate for 12 months.
When do I get paid? +
Growth hosts receive payouts on Stripe's standard rolling schedule — typically 2 business days in the US. Enterprise hosts receive priority T+1 payouts. Community hosts run free events only and do not process payments.
Is there a transaction limit? +
Individual ticket transactions are capped at $25,000 per transaction. There is no cap on total event revenue or total sales volume across your account.
